MateMate – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

MateMate does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely on your device.

1. Introduction

MateMate ("we", "our", or "us") is a math education game developed for children and adults alike. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("the App") available on Google Play.

By using MateMate, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

MateMate is designed to be fully offline and privacy-first. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data (GPS or IP-based)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Any data from children under 13

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

MateMate may save game progress, scores, and settings directly on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third party. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in your device settings.

4. No Internet Connection Required

MateMate does not require an internet connection to function. The App does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or cloud services during normal use.

5. Third-Party Services

MateMate does not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social media plugins, or tracking libraries. No data is shared with third parties.

The App is distributed via the Google Play Store. Google's own privacy practices apply to the download and installation process. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MateMate is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • We do not display targeted or behavioral advertising.
  • We do not require account creation or registration.
  • No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or financial transactions are processed through the App.

Parents and guardians can contact us at any time with questions or concerns about their child's use of the App.

7. Financial Features

MateMate does not include any financial features, in-app purchases, subscriptions, virtual currencies, gambling mechanics, or monetization of any kind. The App is completely free to download and use.

8. Permissions

MateMate requests only the minimum permissions necessary for the App to function. Any permissions requested are used solely for the core functionality of the game and not for data collection purposes.

9. Data Security

Since MateMate does not transmit or store personal data on external servers, the risk of data breaches affecting users is minimal. All locally stored data (game progress, settings) is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of your data (stored locally; deletable by uninstalling the App)
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the App

MateMate – Política de Privacidad

Última actualización: 5 de junio de 2026

MateMate no recopila, almacena ni comparte ninguna información personal. La aplicación funciona completamente en tu dispositivo.

1. Introducción

MateMate ("nosotros", "nuestro") es un juego educativo de matemáticas desarrollado para niños y adultos. Esta Política de Privacidad explica cómo manejamos la información cuando usas nuestra aplicación móvil disponible en Google Play.

2. Información que NO Recopilamos

MateMate está diseñado para ser completamente offline y con privacidad como prioridad. No recopilamos:

  • Nombres, correos electrónicos ni identificadores personales
  • Datos de ubicación (GPS o basados en IP)
  • Identificadores de dispositivo o publicidad
  • Fotos, contactos o archivos de tu dispositivo
  • Análisis de uso o datos de seguimiento de comportamiento
  • Información de pago o financiera
  • Ningún dato de menores de 13 años

3. Datos Almacenados Localmente

MateMate puede guardar el progreso del juego, puntuaciones y configuraciones directamente en tu dispositivo usando almacenamiento local. Estos datos nunca salen de tu dispositivo y no son accesibles por nosotros ni por terceros. Puedes eliminarlos desinstalando la app o borrando los datos de la app en la configuración de tu dispositivo.

4. Sin Conexión a Internet Requerida

MateMate no requiere conexión a internet para funcionar. La app no se comunica con servidores externos, APIs ni servicios en la nube durante el uso normal.

5. Servicios de Terceros

MateMate no integra redes publicitarias de terceros, SDKs de análisis, plugins de redes sociales ni librerías de seguimiento. No se comparten datos con terceros.

6. Privacidad de Menores (Cumplimiento COPPA)

MateMate es adecuado para usuarios de todas las edades, incluidos menores de 13 años. En cumplimiento con COPPA y regulaciones similares:

  • No recopilamos intencionalmente información personal de menores de 13 años.
  • No mostramos publicidad dirigida o conductual.
  • No requerimos creación de cuentas ni registro.
  • No se procesan compras dentro de la app ni transacciones financieras.

7. Características Financieras

MateMate no incluye características financieras, compras dentro de la app, suscripciones, monedas virtuales, mecánicas de apuestas ni monetización de ningún tipo. La app es completamente gratuita para descargar y usar.

8. Cambios a esta Política

Podemos actualizar esta Política de Privacidad ocasionalmente. Cualquier cambio se publicará en esta página con una fecha de revisión actualizada.
